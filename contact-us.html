FAQs

Yes, we offer gluten free treats. All gluten free treats are kept on a separate shelf in store and are clearly marked. If you have any concerns about gluten content within a particular product, please consult one of our onsite bakers.

Yes! We love custom orders for cakes. However, we're unable to offer custom orders for savoury products. If you'd like a custom cake order, please use the contact form above to provide us with the following details, and feel free to attach any reference pictures:
  • The flavour of cake (e.g. vanilla, chocolate, Red Velvet, coffee, etc.)
  • Desired filling (e.g. Strawberry/Raspberry Jam, chocolate/vanilla buttercream, etc.)
  • Any decoration or theme (e.g. Barbie themed, decorated in blue, etc.)
  • Any messages you want written on the cakes
Once we have this information, we'll call or email to confirm your order.

For a custom cake to be ready on time, please ensure you submit your order at least one week prior to the cake being needed.

While we make every effort to control for allergies, we are not an allergen free environment. All allergens are clearly displayed on product labels, and if you have any doubts, please consult one of the onsite bakers. While we do not have the following allergens onsite, we cannot guarantee that the locations our ingredients come from are allergen free.
